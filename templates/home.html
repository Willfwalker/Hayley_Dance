About Me

Dancer. Performer. Creator.

Born and raised in Gulfport, Mississippi, I discovered my passion for dance at an early age. My journey has led me to Belhaven University, where I'm honing my craft and expanding my artistic horizons.

With a strong foundation in ballet, including pointe work, I've developed a versatile style that encompasses the precision of classical techniques and the freedom of contemporary expression. My training in modern and jazz dance has further enriched my movement vocabulary, allowing me to adapt to various performance contexts.

I believe that dance is both a form of self-expression and a powerful means of communication. Through movement, I aim to tell stories that resonate with audiences and create experiences that linger in memory long after the performance ends.

When not in the studio or on stage, I enjoy choreographing original pieces, collaborating with fellow artists, and exploring new dance forms to continuously evolve as a performer.

Dance Specialties
